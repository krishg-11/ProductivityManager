Productivity Manager


What is Productivity Manager?

Productivity Manager is your buddy to help you study better! This application will keep track and notify you of any distractions while you are working. Press the button at the bottom to begin ↓

Eye Tracking

We've implemented an eye tracking system to check if you are dozing off or spacing out. Using the webcam, the application will see if your eyes are in view.

Tab Switching

Getting preoccupied by Youtube or Reddit? We tally the number of times you have switched to another tab while studying.

Phone Detection

It's easy to become distracted by the notifications on your phone by Instagram posts or DMs. We make sure your phone or other mobile devices are out of sight.
